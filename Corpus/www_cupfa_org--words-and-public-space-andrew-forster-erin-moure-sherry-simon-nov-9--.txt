Words & Public Space: What happens when you turn a polylingual novel outward and make it speak to readers on the street?Please join Andrew Forster (artist), Erín Moure (translator and poet) and Sherry Simon (Concordia U, French) for a discussion on words and public space and a presentation of the artifact-book-translation Paraguayan Sea by Wilson Bueno (Nightboat Books, NYC).Date: November 9, 2017
Time: 5 p.m. to 7 p.m.
Location: FOFA Gallery,
Montréal, H3G 2W1
Cost: Admission is freeEvent co-sponsored by CUPFA
