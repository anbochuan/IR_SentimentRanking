Hingston Hall (HA)
A co-ed* residence for 118 full-time undergraduates.
Facilities & funishings
Common kitchenettes lounges on each floor equipped with microwave ovens, toaster, kettle, coffeemaker and cable television
Communal gender-designated bathrooms with independent showers on each floor
Television room with piano, games room with pool table
Laundry facilities with iron and ironing board
Wi-Fi
Single bed (including the mattress)
Two small dressers, mini fridge, desk with chair, armoire
Curtains
Linens, towels, and pillows are not provided
*co-ed means male and female students share common living areas.
