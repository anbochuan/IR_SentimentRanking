Special Collections
Special Collections houses and provides access to an array of rare books, archival materials, maps, photographs, and other
materials that require special handling and preservation because of their rarity, fragility, unusual format, or uniqueness.
About us
Special Collections supports the research needs of the Concordia University community by providing its members with access to
vibrant primary and secondary source materials. The majority of our collections do not circulate and are housed at the
on Concordia's Loyola Campus. Some
specialized materials are integrated with the Libraries circulating collection and may be found at both the
and
Libraries.
