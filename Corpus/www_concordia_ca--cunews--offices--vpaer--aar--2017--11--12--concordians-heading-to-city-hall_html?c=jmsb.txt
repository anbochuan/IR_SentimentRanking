Concordians heading to city hall
Record number of alumni elected or re-elected in municipal elections held across Quebec on November 5, 2017
Posted on November 10, 2017
|
By: Howard Bokser
Source:
More than 40
alumni will be representing their local constituents for the next four years following their victories in the municipal elections held across Quebec on November 5, 2017.
The following are the newly elected or returning city representatives — including six mayors — listed by their municipalities:
Alex Bottausci was one of six Concordia alumni elected mayor in the province-wide municipal elections on November 5, 2017. Bottausci is mayor of Dollard-des-Ormeaux.
