Career Management Services
Career Management Services
John Molson School of Business’ Career Management Services (CMS) aims to empower students and alumni by providing resources, programs and networking opportunities to effectively achieve professional success. CMS also strives to cultivate and sustain productive relationships with our recruitment partners in the business community.
For students
Access our job posting site, register for career events, browse career planning tools & more.
For employers
Discover recruitment options, post jobs, showcase your company at career fairs and more.
For alumni
Access our job posting site, register for career events, browse online career material & more.
Get to know us
Our philosophy is based on value creation to help students and alumni reach professional success. The CMS team is also dedicated to building sustainable relationships with our partners in the business community.
