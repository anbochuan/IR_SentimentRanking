Welcome Centre
The Welcome Centre is a great starting point for prospective students, family members, and visitors.
At the Welcome Centre, you can:
Learn more about Concordia
Arrange your campus tour
Get answers to questions about the admissions process
Be directed to locations, services or events on campus
Where to find us
The Welcome Centre is on the main floor of the Library Building.
1400 De Maisonneuve Blvd. W.,
()
We’re open Monday to Friday, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
514-848-2424, ext. 4779
Campus tours
Let a current student guide you in discovering our two campuses and student life in Montreal. Your tour includes a free shuttle bus ride between the two campuses, about 20 minutes each way. Parents and friends are welcome to join too! All tours leave from the Welcome Centre.
