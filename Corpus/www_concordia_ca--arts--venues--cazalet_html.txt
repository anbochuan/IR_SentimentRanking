Cazalet Theatre
Cazalet Theatre
The Cazalet Theatre, located on the Loyola Campus, is a flexible black-box venue that can seat 60-70 people.
It hosts many different types of productions, from musicals to multi-media and interdisciplinary events, classes and workshops, and has become home to the Department of Theatre's Student Initiated Production Assignments (SIPAs).
