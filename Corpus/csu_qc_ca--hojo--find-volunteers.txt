Post your ad with us for FREE! Just follow these steps1. Go to our  and click on the classifieds banner2. Click “Register” at the top of the screen3. Fill out the necessary information (no student ID) and click register4. Click “Post an Ad” or “New Ad” at the top of the screen5. Select the appropriate category, then sub-category6. Click the blue box "Add Ad to this Category"7. Fill out the form8. Click "Register" at the end of the form9. Once your ad is validated by our staff, you will receive a notification email.
