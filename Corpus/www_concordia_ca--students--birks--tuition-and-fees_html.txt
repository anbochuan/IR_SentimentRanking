Tuition and fees
Tuition and fees
You have many payment choices
You will likely find it faster and more efficient . You are always welcome to visit Birks to pay your tuition or get advice.
To pay tuition at Birks, bring your student ID and one of the following methods of payment:
personal or certified cheque
Money order
Bank draft
Debit card (Interac)
The University offers several ways of making payment without coming to Birks Student Service Centre. For a complete list please visit the tuition & fees website's .
