Immigration Quebec Information Session
December 1, 2017, 3 p.m. – 5:30 p.m.
Are you currently living in Québec as an international student and would like to settle here permanently?
Find out about the possibilities available to you by participating in an information session concerning the immigration procedures offered by the Ministère de l’Immigration, de la Diversité et de l’Inclusion, in collaboration with Montréal International.
We have some guest speakers who will present information to you,  a Question and Answer period will follow the presentation. To make the most of this session take a look at  program specifically for international students.
NOTE: The ISO staff cannot act as a student’s representative or provide advising for CSQ or permanent residency applications.
