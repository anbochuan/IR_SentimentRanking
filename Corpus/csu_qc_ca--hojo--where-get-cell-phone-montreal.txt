Looking for a cellphone provider in Montreal? Here are a list of several cell phone providers. You can find stores near your location by going to their website. Always make sure to consult several different providers to find the rates and phone best for you, remember to read any contract in full before signing it so you know what you are committing to. Please note: we do not recommend any provider and cannot give advice on which carrier to use.
