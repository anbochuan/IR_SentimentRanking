Payroll
Some of the services Payroll provides are:
Provide accurate and timely payments to all University faculty, staff and students.
Deduct, remit and report on statutory taxes (based on Federal and Provincial laws), benefit deductions, union dues and other deductions.
Provide information and explanations to members of the Concordia community in an honest and professional manner.
