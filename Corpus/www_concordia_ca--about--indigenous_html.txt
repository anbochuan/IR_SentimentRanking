Wa'tkwanonhwerá:ton is a formal greeting in Kanien'kéha (Mohawk language) that means, 'Our minds greet yours.'

Shiann Whitebean, 2017 Concordia Valedictorian"We Concordians push boundaries and exceed limits, but that is how we revolutionize and challenge society.  That is what leaders and innovators do."
A founding member of the Indigenous Directions Leadership Group (IDLG),  was one of eight Concordia students chosen to be valedictorian at June Convocation, 2017. Listening to her speech, it is clear that they made the right choice.
