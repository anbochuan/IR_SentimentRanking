Academic integrity
Academic integrity
Concordia University places the principle of academic integrity, that is, honesty, responsibility and fairness in all aspects of academic life, as one of its highest values.
Instructors, students and administrators are expected to be honest and responsible in their academic conduct and fair in their assessment of academic matters.
