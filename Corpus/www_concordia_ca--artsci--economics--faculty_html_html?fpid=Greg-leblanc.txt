Greg LeBlanc, PhD
Chair and Associate Professor, Economics
Office:
Henry F. Hall Building,1455 De Maisonneuve W.
Phone:

Education
PhD (Queen's University)
Fields of specialization
Industrial Organization and Labour Economics.
Research interests
Strategic behavior under uncertainty in the following contexts: self-enforced labour market contracts, inter-firm competition, and corporate governance.
Professional experience
Has taught at Concordia University since completing his Ph.D in 1990. Taught courses at the undergraduate and graduate levels in Industrial and Labour Economics, and conducts research in these areas. He is currently engaged in a three-year research project entitled "Predatory Business Practices" funded by the SSHRC and the FCAR.
