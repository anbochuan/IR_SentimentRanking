About the Work-study program
The Work–study program helps fund part-time jobs on campus for students who are in financial need.  Eligible students may work up to 20 hours per week to a maximum of 200 hours per semester in administrative, research or technical positions.  Jobs are subsidized by the ministère de l’Éducation et Enseignement supérieure and Concordia and pay between $11.25 and $13.00 per hour.
