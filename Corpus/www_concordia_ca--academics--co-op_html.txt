Institute for Co-operative Education
Co-operative education bridges university life and the working world. For 36 years, our model of experiential learning has alternated study with work-terms. Students test drive their careers and employers access more than 1,800 highly skilled students in over 40 programs.

Every year, Co-op connects about 1,800 students to hundreds of employers through 40 undergraduate and four graduate co-op programs.
