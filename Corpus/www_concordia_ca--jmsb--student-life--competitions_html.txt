Case competition program
The John Molson School of Business (JMSB) learning experience focuses on innovative educational practices that combine traditional classroom learning with real-life knowledge application. Business competitions give students an additional opportunity to integrate these two elements. JMSB students are recognized worldwide for their ability to analyze and provide solutions to complex business problems in an increasingly global business environment.
