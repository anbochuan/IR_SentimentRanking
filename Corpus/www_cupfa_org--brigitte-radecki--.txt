Featured MemberBrigitte RadeckiBrigitte Radecki has exhibited throughout  Canada and  Europe for over thirty years. Born in Germany, she received both her BFA and  MFA (1982) from Concordia and now lives and teaches in Montreal. Initially working in sculpture and installation, she has had solo exhibitions at Musée d’art contemporain in Montréal and has shown regularly at Gallery Christiane Chassay. She has also participated in several group exhibitions in Europe, Quebec, British Columbia and Ontario. Since  the early 1990’sshe has concentrated entirely on abstract painting installations.  Her work is in several public and private collections such as  the Musée des beaux-arts de Montréal, Musée d’art contemporain in Montréal,  the Musée National des beaux-arts du Québec, Air Canada and  the National Bank.
