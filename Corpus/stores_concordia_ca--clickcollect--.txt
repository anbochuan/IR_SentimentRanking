Click & Collect
click & collect
This free service is available all year round!
The Free pick up in store option allows you to order online and pick up your purchase in store at the Click & Collect counter.
Orders will be fulfilled and ready for pick-up within two business days. When your order is ready to be picked-up you will receive an email notice.
1. Place your order
2. Select "Click & Collect" at checkout
3. You will receive an email notice when your order is ready
4. Pick-up your order at the click and collect counter
Books must be picked up from the campus where the class is taken. If you are taking courses at the downtown (SGW) campus, your books will be available at the SGW Book Stop (LB-03). If you are taking courses at the Loyola campus, your books will be available at the Loyola Book Stop (CJ-1.422).
During the rush period,
you will be allowed to bypass the main lineup.
The Click & Collect counter is located inside the Book Stop. Show your pick-up confirmation email to a customer
representative, look for a short Click & Collect line or go straight to the entrance for immediate Click & Collect access.
Procedure
In order to pick-up your order at the Click & Collect counter present:
Identification (student or photo ID)
A copy of your pick-up confirmation email
(printed or digital)
If you are not able to present the required documents we will not be able to complete your order.
Orders not picked up
Due to space availability any orders that have chosen the shipping option of “Click & Collect” must be picked up, within 10 business days of purchase. Failure to pick up an order within 10 days will result in the order being refunded to the credit card used for the original purchase, minus a $10 processing fee (except non-refundable special order Coursepacks). Special order Coursepacks not picked up within 15 days will be destroyed with no refund issued.
