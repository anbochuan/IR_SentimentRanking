Hingston Hall
(HB)
A co-ed* residence for 118 full-time undergraduates.
Facilities & funishings
Common kitchenettes and lounges on each floor, equipped with microwave ovens, toaster, kettle, coffeemaker and cable television
Communal private gender designated bathrooms with independent showers on each floor
Games room with pool table
Laundry facilities with iron and ironing board
Wi-Fi
Single bed in single rooms
Double bed in large single rooms
Dresser, mini fridge, desk and chair, armoire
Ceiling fans in each room
Curtains
Linens, towels, and pillows are not provided
*co-ed means male and female students share common living areas, including bathrooms.
