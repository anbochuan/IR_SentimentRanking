MBA in Investment Management, with CFA integration - Information Session (online and in Montreal)
November 21, 2017, 6 p.m. – 7 p.m.
This information session will cover all aspects of the
including an overview of the program, application process, admission requirements, curriculum and career opportunities, plus insight into the overall learning experience. Guest alumnus , MBA, CFA, Senior Analyst - Public Markets at BRK Capital, will be sharing his experience in the program and where it has lead him.
Please note, this session will also be live-streamed over Adobe Connect's interactive webinar platform for those who cannnot be at the session in-person in Montreal. Registered participants will be sent the access link via e-mail shortly before the session begins.
