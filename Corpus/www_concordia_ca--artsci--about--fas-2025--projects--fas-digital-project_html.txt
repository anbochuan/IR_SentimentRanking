Your browser does not support the video tag.THE FAS DIGITAL PROJECT
The Digital Project explores novel methods of integrating digital approaches to research, teaching and learning. Investigating new digital platforms will make our current and future scholars more accessible to an audience beyond the university and showcase our talents.
We are looking for your ideas in the following areas:
testing the student digital portfolio concept
developing and testing ideas for Massive Online Open Courses (MOOCs) that enhance our reputation and presence in the academic arena on the world stage
piloting digital lab platforms
embedding digital labs in a renewed curriculum at all levels
planning optimal integration of digital approaches and resources into the classroom
enhancing the integration of digital learning into the student experience
