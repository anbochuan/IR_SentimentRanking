PROGRAMS &STUDY OPTIONS
Graduate programs
Join a community of graduate students who question the status quo and push their work beyond disciplinary boundaries in over 120 graduate programs.
Professional programs
We offer over 30 professional programs across four faculties, allowing you to expand your professional network, hone your skills and launch your career.
Interdisciplinary programs
Students at Concordia have multiple ways of breaking disciplinary boundaries through a variety of interfaculty and intersecting programs and initiatives.
Individualized programs
Pursue specific research goals outside our existing graduate programs. INDI programs give you access to resources within and across faculties so you can develop a deeper understanding of a research problem.
University Teaching program
Design your own program and explore your passions in an environment that provides enough structure to stay on track.
Inter-university agreements
Create international collaborations that strengthen the quality of your PhD research. Cotutelle allows you to pursue a doctoral degree at Concordia and a partner institution, giving you exposure to diverse educational and cultural practices.
Open courses
Broaden your graduate experience by taking a course in a program with cognate interests. Consult the open courses list for ways to apply your disciplinary expertise to another academic area.
Summer courses
Take part in an interdisciplinary seminar led by acclaimed faculty members from Concordia and other universities. Experiential learning activities let you experiment with cutting-edge research.
