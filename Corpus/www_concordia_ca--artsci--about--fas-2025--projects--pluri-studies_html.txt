Your browser does not support the video tag.PLURI-STUDIES INSTITUTE
The Pluri-Studies Institute is a think tank whose goal is to execute pilot projects that expand upon the established curriculum. These projects will facilitate the testing of new approaches to research and pedagogy and highlight innovative FAS projects.
PSI is a springboard to get your initiative off the ground. Take advantage of:
a space to test ideas around interdisciplinary research and curriculum
a hub for collaborative experimentation and a testing ground for new ideas and methodologies
a convener of faculty and students from around the world to advance knowledge
a facilitator of potential connections across departments and units
