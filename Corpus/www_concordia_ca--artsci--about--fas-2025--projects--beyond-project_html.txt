THE BEYOND PROJECT
The Beyond Project expands our community engagement by bringing interdisciplinary interventions to real-world problems. This project is an opportunity to create strong ties with organizations that will strengthen our mission at the national and international levels.
We are looking for proposals that will:
optimize community engagement initiatives
engaging in meaningful and deep relationships with selected community partners
connect with external organizations to  develop and establish international graduate schools
test and launch field schools with other Faculties
