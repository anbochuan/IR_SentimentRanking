PhD Graduate Kris Woofter Named Valedictorian for Fall 2017 Convocation
Posted on November 6, 2017
Kris Woofter, 2017 Valedictorian
The Mel Hoppenheim School of Cinema is proud to announce that Dr. Kris Woofter has been named valedictorian for the Fall 2017 convocation!
A recent graduate of the PhD Film and Moving Image Studies program, Kris successfully defended his thesis in September of 2016. The thesis, “Shoot the Dead: Horror Cinema, Documentary and Gothic Realism”, can be found .
Congratulations, Dr. Woofter!
