Undergraduate
The Faculty of Engineering and Computer Science has a number of undergraduate entrance awards that are listed on the . Awards are usually available for September entry students.
Once the March 1 application deadline passes, top applicants are contacted and offered these awards. Conditions for each award may vary. As these are merit-based awards, there is no need to apply. Qualifying students will be automatically contacted.
