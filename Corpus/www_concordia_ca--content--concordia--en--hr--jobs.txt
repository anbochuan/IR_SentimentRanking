Working at Concordia
Work in an inspiring and challenging place. Engage with big questions head-on. Search for innovative solutions and make positive contributions to society. Concordia creativity powers Montreal and connects us to the world. Work with us to redefine the 21st century university.
Why work at Concordia?
With more than 7,000 faculty and staff, Concordia University enables individuals to grow, contribute and innovate whatever their career. We support and develop aspirations, bridge communities and help enrich world knowledge. We offer:
A highly diverse community in one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the world.
A leading-edge teaching and research environment.
Big thinking. We addresses societal issues from a broad perspective and seize opportunities with diversity of thought and openness of spirit.
Competitive compensation and benefits, opportunities for continuous learning and development, and a stimulating and dynamic work environment.
