John Dobson Investment Room
John Dobson – Formula Growth Investment Room
Welcome to the John Dobson – Formula Growth Investment Room at the John Molson School of Business (JMSB). This lab was founded in 2000 with the mission of using industry tools and live financial software, by providing students practical knowledge of the markets and equipping them with a competitive edge in their careers.
The lab is home to 10 Bloomberg Terminals and a total of 26 regular PCs. As well as having more than 17 databases and eight specific software the John Dobson – Formula Growth Investment Room is ideal to answer researchers and students’ needs.
The lab is an area where students can come to apply what they learn in classes. From creating their own models, running their own portfolio, or finish an assignment, this is the place to go. Professors and teachers may also use the room to improve the teaching experience.
Come visit us to experience first-hand the benefits of using the John Dobson – Formula Growth Investment Room. The lab is located in MB 12.254.
