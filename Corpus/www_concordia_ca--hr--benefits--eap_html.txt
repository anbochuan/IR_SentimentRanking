Employee Assistance Program
Employee Assistance Program
Concordia's Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is a strictly voluntary, confidential counselling, referral and information service that provides a broad range of services to employees eligible for Health Benefits and their immediate family. Services in English and French are available to you at no cost 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
