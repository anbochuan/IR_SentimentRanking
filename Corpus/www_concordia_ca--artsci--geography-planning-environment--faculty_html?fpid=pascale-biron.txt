Faculty
Pascale Biron, PhD
Professor and Chair, Geography, Planning and Environment
Office:
Henry F. Hall Building,1455 De Maisonneuve W.
Phone:
(514) 848-2424 ext. 2061
Email:
Dr. Biron has studied at the Université de Montréal and at Leeds
University during her doctoral research on fluvial geomorphology. She has joined
Concordia in 1998. Her research involves river dynamics, stream restoration
for fish habitat, sustainable management of agricultural streams, climate change
impacts on rivers, three-dimensional computational fluid dynamics and river
management. Dr. Biron works in collaboration with researchers in Biology and
Earth Science departments.
