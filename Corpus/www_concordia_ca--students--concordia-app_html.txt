THE CONCORDIA APPOn your phone, at your fingertips
The Concordia app, designed to enhance your university experience, is now available for download.Get your schedule and grades instantly, consult campus maps, track the shuttle bus to plan your trips between campuses, connect with the library, and figure out what to eat and where.
This version of the app is only phase one. User feedback regarding functionality will be key to ensuring the app continues to meet the needs of the student community, as will suggestions on future components. Fill out this online survey so we can continually improve the Concordia app.
My Account
Consult your class schedules, cancellations and grades.
Maps
Access detailed maps of the Loyola and Sir George Williams campuses.
Shuttle bus
Plan your trip between campuses with shuttle bus schedules and the live tracker.
Safety
Get the information you need to stay safe on campus.
Directories
Access faculty and staff contact information.

Access the library database, e-journals, and chat with a librarian.
Food
Find out where to eat on and around campus.

Important dates

Stores
Connect with the Book Stop, Book Stop Boutique, Print Store and Art Supply Store.
Tips
Get advice on how to succeed academically, stay healthy and find awesome things to do off and on campus.

This feature includes a Concordia-related Read of the Day.
