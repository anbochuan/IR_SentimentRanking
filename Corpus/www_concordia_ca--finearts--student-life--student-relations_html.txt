Office of Student Relations
The Office of Student Relations is a resource for all Fine Arts students, faculty and staff interested in accessing or knowing more about Fine Arts student life activities, including arts related governance, gallery direction, festival production, resource centre management and non-profit arts related businesses.
In addition to cultivating links between the wider student population and extra-curricular student-run projects such as , Café X,
and the , the Office of Student Relations liaises with student-run organizations to help ensure the success of these projects in an arm’s length capacity and can be called upon by the
as an impartial arm’s length resource at the request of FASA or those participating in its programs.
