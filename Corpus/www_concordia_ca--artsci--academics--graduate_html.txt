Graduate programs
Concordia's Faculty of Arts and Science is a powerhouse of interdisciplinarity that embraces bold thinking in research, teaching and learning. Rooted in academic disciplines across the humanities, the social sciences and the sciences our diversity sparks new ways of imagining the academic and research experience.
