Student Success Centre
Student Success Centre
We empower students to maximize their personal, academic, and career potential. We provide diverse programs, resources and expertise in a supportive student-centred environment to facilitate student success.
Our services
Looking for part-time jobs, internships or full time jobs? The Career and Planning Services (CAPS) Office has the resources you need to help you research careers, get ready for the job market, and connect with employers.
Do you know how to do well in your courses? Our team of Learning Specialists, Peer Tutors, Study Group Leaders and Conversation Facilitators help you make the best of your academic potential. All students—undergraduate, graduate, independent and continuing education—welcome.
Check out an orientation, connect with a student success mentor, or read our handy reference guides for new students. There are many ways to get the support you need.
Learn about your new campus, meet new people and find out what it takes to be a successful student. Get informed. Get connected. Get it right. Drop by the Welcome Crew Office and speak to a mentor today.
Request a mentor
Become a mentor
Get the answers and help you need when facing difficult situations. The Student Advocacy Office is here to assist you and help you understand Concordia’s rules and regulations, the academic code of conduct and academic integrity.
