Facilities & services
Facilities & services
JMSB offers its students a comprehensive assortment of resources and services to ensure that they have the tools to be successful in their studies and in their careers.
What we offer:
Career Management Services
For students
Access our job posting site, register for career events, browse career planning tools & more.
John Molson School of Business Building
The John Molson School of Business Building is a 37,000-square-metre, 15-storey landmark boasting a distinctive design that incorporates bright atriums, modern classrooms, networking spaces, LEED certification and more.
Career Management Services
For employers
Discover recruitment options, post jobs, showcase your company at career fairs and more.
John Dobson—Formula Growth Investment Room
Home to 10 Bloomberg Terminals, 26 regular PCs, 17 databases and 8 specific software programs, the John Dobson—Formula Growth Investment Room is an indispensable tool for both students and researchers.
Centre for Instructional Technology
Find technical support, educational technology, systems and network services, and multi-media support.
Computers, printing & study space
JMSB offers computer labs, high-quality laser printers, free newspapers, phones and plenty of dedicated study space.
Embedded in the community
To better serve the needs of Montreal's business community, the John Molson School of Business engages alumni and industry as partners in developing high quality, innovative programs that meet their evolving needs.
