Concordia University Alumni Association
The Board of Directors of the  (CUAA) is responsible for all the association's finances, policies and activities.
The Board holds four evening meetings and one annual general meeting per year. Board members are invited to chair or sit on one or more committees and to participate in one strategic planning session per year. Directors are encouraged to represent the CUAA at various alumni and student events.
