PROJECTED FUTURESWhat is the future of science journalism?
August 7 – 11, 2017
Concordia's  and in partnership with the  and the , offers an intensive and experiential summer school that challenges participants to rethink how science is communicated within society.
With an emphasis on robust, evidence-based science journalism, Projected Futures: Experimental Science Journalism Studies  sparks interdisciplinary experimentation to contemplate and create new forms of scientific storytelling.
