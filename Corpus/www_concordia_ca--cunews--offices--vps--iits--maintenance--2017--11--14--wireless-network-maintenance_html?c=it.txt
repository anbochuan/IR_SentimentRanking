Wireless network maintenance: Nov 14
Posted on November 14, 2017
Maintenance affecting Concordia's ConcordiaUniversity, Eduroam, and ConcordiaGuest wireless networks is scheduled to be performed on November 15, 2017 from 5:00 a.m. until 6:00 a.m.
During the maintenance window, wireless users will lose connection for approximately 5 minutes.
If you have questions about the maintenance period or service interruption, contact the Service Desk by email at
or call 514-848-2424, ext. 7613 to speak with a Service Desk agent between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. on weekdays.
