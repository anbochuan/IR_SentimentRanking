Publications & reports
Concordia University Magazine
Concordia University Magazine is published three times a year for alumni and friends of Concordia University. Have the print format mailed to you, or access the digital version.
eNewsletters
NOW Newsletter
The NOW newsletter is the official source of news and events at Concordia. During the academic year, a staff/faculty edition is sent out every Monday and Wednesday; there are student editions on Tuesday (for graduate students) and Thursday (for undergraduates). A media edition goes out on Thursday; and there's a public edition every second Friday.
Accent
The Accent e-newsletter is geared to alumni and is published monthly. Have Concordia news delivered conveniently to your inbox to stay informed. [Monthly]
The President’s Report
The President’s Report presents the highlights of the past academic year under the theme, Next-Generation University. [Annual]
Concordia: An Economic Force Connected to its Community
Concordia: An Economic Force Connected to its Community is a study on the impact of Concordia University published by SECOR. [Periodically]

Concordia Library Annual Report 2015-16

Other publications
Viewbook
The Viewbook is an introduction to student life at Concordia, and our four amazing Faculties with information on the admissions and the application process. [Periodically]
Graduate Programs Viewbook
The Graduate Programs Viewbook presents the advantages of graduate study at Concordia for both specialized research and transdisciplinary study, along with information on admissions and the application process. [Periodically]
