Student Academic Services
(SAS)
SAS is the place to go to if you are a student in the Faculty of Arts and Science with questions about your program, registration, exchange opportunities, academic difficulties, or just to confirm that you are on the right track with your degree.
Undergraduate academic advising guide 2017-2018
This guide contains an explanation of academic terms, instructions on how to read your student record, regulations on academic performance, information on language placement tests and much more!
What we do
SAS is responsible for admissions to and general advising for students in the Faculty of Arts and Science.
Speak to an academic counselor.
Get advice about university regulations.
Obtain the forms you need to make changes to your program or your courses.
Be directed to the various counseling services, as appropriate.
Discuss any academic problem.
Be encouraged to reach your own academic success!
