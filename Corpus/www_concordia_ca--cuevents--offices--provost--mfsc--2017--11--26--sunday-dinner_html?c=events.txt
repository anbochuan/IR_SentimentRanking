Sunday Dinner
November 26, 2017, 6 p.m. – 9 p.m.
What could be better?
Free dinner, delicious vegetarian food, great community and lots of engaging conversations. If you want to be part of this, request your ticket!  As this event is extremely popular, we reserve 5 tickets for first-timers, 5 tickets for people willing to help with the preparations, and the rest of the tickets will be given away through a draw.
Register by November 20, 10 am and wait to hear if you are one of the lucky ticket winners!
