Independent student advising
Independent student advising
Admission and academic advising is available through:
Appropriate Faculty
Current Independent students interested in applying to a degree and/or taking courses may obtain admission advice from the appropriate faculty:
Main Telephone Number: 514-848-2424
Arts and Science – ext. 2104
Engineering & Computer Science – ext. 3055
Fine Arts – ext. 4612
John Molson School of Business – ext. 2721 |
Enrolment Services
To meet an Admissions Information Officer to discuss general admissions information, please call 514-848-2424 ext. 2668 to make an appointment.
The Admissions Advisor, Independent Students (undergraduate) is the designated person responsible for Independent Students. Please call 514-848-2424 ext. 2614 should need to discuss a specific issue with your independent application or current independent studies.
For advice on Graduate Independent Studies please contact the Program Assistant of the graduate program which is offering the course. .
