Ailie Cleghorn, PhD
Professor, Education
Office:
Faubourg Ste-Catherine Building,1610 St. Catherine W.
Phone:
(514) 848-2424 ext. 2041
Email:
Dr. Cleghorn’s area of expertise is Comparative Sociology of Education.
Her research centers on African and other still-developing primary and
pre-primary school settings with special regard to language of
instruction, science education, the use of text materials, and
cross-cultural conceptions of childhood. She teaches Comparative
Education at the undergraduate level; at the graduate level she teaches
cross-cultural perspectives on literacy, and research methods in
education.
Current Projects
Language and literacy in diverse school settings
Cultural conceptions of childhood: India, South Africa, Canada
Global influences on teaching in early years education
Courses taught
Comparative and International Education
Literacy and development: cross cultural perspectives
Introduction to research methods in education
