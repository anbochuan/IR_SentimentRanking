Scholarships and Bursaries
If you have been awarded a scholarship or bursary, it will normally be paid to you via your student account.  After you receive an email confirmation that your scholarship or bursary has been disbursed, you can come to Birks to:
Verify that the correct amount has been deposited
Drop off your thank you letter
Awards, Bursary and Work-Study applications
Applications and/or supporting documents for these programs can be dropped off at Birks or brought directly to the .
