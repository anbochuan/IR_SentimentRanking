Off the Shelf in Sherbrooke: Rod MacLeod & Heather Darch
November 23, 2017, 7 p.m. – 9 p.m.
This event looks at stories involving both conflict and community-building in Quebec's English-speaking community. Heather Darch, curator of the , will look at the impact of the Fenian Raids of 1866 and 1870 on the border regions of Quebec. Rod MacLeod, who co-authored the , will ponder the heritage and continuing challenges surrounding English-language schools in the province, which are key community cornerstones.
