Applied Psychology Centre (APC)
Applied Psychology Centre (APC)
The Applied Psychology Centre (APC) is the in-house training clinic of the department. The APC provides therapy, counselling and assessment services to the local community.
We offer the following services:
Therapy, psychological and school evaluations for children, adolescents, adults and families.
Neuropsychological evaluations for adults.
Psychological services Psychology Interns and Psychologist.
Services are available September to April by appointment only
The clinic is open Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm and Wednesday evening until 7pm.
