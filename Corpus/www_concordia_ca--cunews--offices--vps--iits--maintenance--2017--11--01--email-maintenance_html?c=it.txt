Faculty & staff email maintenance: Nov 03
Posted on November 1, 2017
Concordia's IT department will be performing maintenance on the Microsoft Exchange Server (faculty & staff email service) on November 03, 2017 between 6:00 p.m. and 9:00 p.m. During the maintenance period, users may experience service interruptions when accessing their mailboxes using email clients, owa.concordia.ca, or mobile devices.
If you have any questions about the maintenance period or service interruption, contact the Service Desk by email at  or call 514-848-2424, ext. 7613 to speak with a Service Desk agent between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. on weekdays.
