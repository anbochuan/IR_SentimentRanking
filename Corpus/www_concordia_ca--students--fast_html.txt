Frontline Administrative Service Team (F.A.S.T)
We want your experience at Concordia to be successful and the start of the academic year is always an exciting and busy time for new and returning Concordia students. You may have such questions as: How can I pay my tuition? Where can I obtain my student ID card? How do I request a Permanent Code?
Throughout the year, you can get answers to these questions – and many more – at the  in the , room .
