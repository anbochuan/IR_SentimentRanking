TOURS & EVENTSThere are many ways to meet us, both on- and off-campus, in Montreal or in your city. Campus tours, Open House, information sessions... Our doors are always open.
Welcome Centre
The Welcome Centre is a great starting point for prospective students, family members, and visitors. Drop by the Welcome Centre to:
Learn more about Concordia
Arrange your campus tour
Get answers to questions about the admissions process
Get directions to locations, services or events on campus
Events and info sessionsOpen House and Portfolio Day are held in the fall and in February. Our recruitment team also visits high schools and attends education fairs across Canada and around the world. If you can’t make it to campus in person, check our travel schedule to see when we plan to visit your area.
Recruitment team
Reach out to one of our recruiters to ask a question about our programs, admissions process or student life in Montreal.
