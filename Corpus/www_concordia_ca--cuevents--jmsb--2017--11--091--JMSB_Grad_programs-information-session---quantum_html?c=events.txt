GMAT Boot Camp - hosted by Quantum Test Prep in Montreal
November 17, 2017 – November 20, 2017, 9 a.m. – 10 p.m.
Concordia University's John Molson School of Business (JMSB), in collaboration with Quantum Test Prep, is hosting a two-weekend GMAT bootcamp from Friday, November 10th to Monday, November 13th and Friday, November 17th to Monday, November 20th.
Most
require a GMAT for admission. This GMAT bootcamp, designed and taught by expert GMAT instructors from Quantum Test Prep, will give you the opportunity to intensely prepare for the GMAT exam.
Concordia GMAT Bootcamp
Concordia University’s John Molson School of Business has partnered with , one of the leading GMAT companies in Canada, to offer an intensive two-weekend Concordia GMAT Bootcamp prep course in Montreal. This 50hr workshop is open to anyone who is interested in taking the GMAT, whether they are planning to attend Concordia or not.
This course provides students with a summary review of all the major GMAT exam sections (Math, Verbal, Integrated Reasoning and Analytical Writing). Classes are held over two consecutive weekends (Friday to Monday); see schedule below.
