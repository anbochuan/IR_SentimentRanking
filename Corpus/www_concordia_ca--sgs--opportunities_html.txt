SKILLS & OPPORTUNITIES
GradProSkills
In partnership with on-campus and external experts, Concordia's Graduate and Professional Skills (GradProSkills) offers over 300 workshops to help graduate students successfully transition into their careers.
Public Scholars Program
Ten highly qualified PhD students selected across our four faculties have been prepared to connect and share their knowledge with the wider community.
Horizon Postdocs
The Horizon Fellowships program brings a fresh cohort of exceptional young researchers to challenge our faculty, mentor the next generation, and boost our research and creative productivity.
Graduate Community Building Fund
The Graduate Community Building Fund provides financial support toward building an intellectual community within graduate programs, by bringing together students and faculty for events, lectures, symposia, and more.
Summer Institute
This interdisciplinary week-long summer class is a unique chance for you to go beyond the classroom, put theory into practice and challenge the status quo.
