Volunteering
The LIVE Centre is Concordia's on-campus Volunteer Resource Centre, your one-stop shop for information about volunteerism on and off campus.
Many Concordia students enrich their university experience by volunteering. Volunteering allows you to support a cause you care about and gain valuable experience.
