Meet our faculty
John Molson School of Business faculty members belong to one of five academic departments. Each department page lists the full-time members of the department and links to their profiles, which describe their current and previous research interests, publications and provides their contact information.
Depending on a student's chosen path of study you will learn from our top academic researchers who enrich the curriculum with their knowledge and experience.
In total, there are 134 full-time faculty from 26 countries, who are able to provide a global perspective to your professional business education. Combine this with the expertise brought to you by the 173 part-time faculty, you will find a well-rounded, real-world inclination to your studies.
