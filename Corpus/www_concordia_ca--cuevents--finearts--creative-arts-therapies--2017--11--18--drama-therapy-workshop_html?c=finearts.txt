Drama Therapy Workshop
November 18, 2017, 10 a.m. – 1 p.m.
An Introduction to Drama Therapy
Concordia University’s Department of Creative Arts Therapies is pleased to extend an invitation to attend its Drama Therapy workshop to all those interested in exploring the field of drama therapy.
This workshop is intended for university, Cegep, or high school students, as well as all others interested in learning more about Drama Therapy educational and career possibilities. Concordia is the only university in Canada to offer a degree in drama therapy.
Workshop Coordinators
Bonnie Harnden, MA, RDT, Associate Professor and Coordinator
Prof. Harnden is a trained drama therapist, psychoanalyst and psychotherapist with over 20 years of experience in clinical practice, research, and training. She specializes in trauma, and family therapy.
Bill Wood, drama therapy student, is completing his internship at the Jewish General Hospital.
