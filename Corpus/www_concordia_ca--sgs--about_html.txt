ABOUT THE SCHOOLThe School of Graduate Studies guides students, faculty and administrators through the academic and administrative components of Concordia’s graduate programs.
Council of the School of Graduate Studies (CSGS)
The CSGS is the School’s governing body, which legislates matters related to graduate programs as per the .
Graduate Curriculum Committee (GCC)
The Graduate Curriculum Committee is a permanent sub-committee of the council of the School of Graduate Studies. The GCC reviews and recommends to Senate all graduate curriculum change proposals submitted by the Faculties.
Graduate Awards Committee
The Concordia Graduate Awards Committee as overall responsibility for administering Concordia University Graduate Fellowships.
