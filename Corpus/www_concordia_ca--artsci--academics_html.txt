ACADEMICS
The Faculty of Arts and Science offers a lot of flexibility in planning your program. Mix and match a major and a minor, complete a double major, or a specialization and two minors — the possibilities are almost endless!
Interdisciplinary studies
For students whose interests do not conform to standard academic programs, interdisciplinarity is the solution. From programs on a particular topic, to individual courses, to an individually-structured program, the Faculty of Arts and Science offers the flexibility you are looking for in your chosen field of study.
Co-operative education
Test drive your career with a co-operative education: By alternating study terms with full-time, paid internships through the Institute for Co-operative Education, you will acquire on-the-job experience, giving you an advantage over other graduating students in a competitive job market.
