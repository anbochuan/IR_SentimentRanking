Student Advocacy Office
The Student Advocacy Office* assists you whenever you face difficult situations and helps you understand Concordia’s rules and regulations. We have been offering free, confidential, services for 23 years to both undergraduate and graduate students.
*Formerly the Student Advocate Program.
The Student Advocacy Office has moved!Our new SAO office is located on the third floor of the Guy-De Maisonneuve Building in GM 320.
